Our Rafting Trips

From serene floats for the whole family to challenging, multi-day adventures for the experienced rafter, we have a trip for everyone. Explore our options below and find your next unforgettable experience on the water.

Futaleufú River Expedition (Class IV-V)

Our most epic and challenging trip. This multi-day expedition takes you through the internationally-renowned whitewater of the Futaleufú River. Experience thrilling rapids, breathtaking scenery, and riverside camping under the stars. This trip requires prior rafting experience.

  • **Duration:** 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • **Level:** Advanced
  • **Price:** $500 per person
Book Now

Family Fun Float (Class I-II)

A perfect, relaxing day on the water for families and beginners. This trip features calm stretches of water, gentle currents, and stunning views of the surrounding wildlife. Our guides will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all ages.

  • **Duration:** Half-Day (Approx. 4 hours)
  • **Level:** Beginner
  • **Price:** $75 per person
Book Now

Trancura River Thrills (Class III-IV)

Get your adrenaline pumping with this action-packed half-day trip. The Trancura River offers a perfect mix of exciting rapids and calmer pools, making it a great choice for those looking for a thrill without the multi-day commitment.

  • **Duration:** Half-Day (Approx. 3 hours)
  • **Level:** Intermediate
  • **Price:** $120 per person
Book Now
