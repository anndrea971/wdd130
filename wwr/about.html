Maipu

Maipú is a commune located in the western area of Santiago, Chile, and one of the most populated in the country. It combines residential neighborhoods with a strong commercial and service activity. It stands out for the Votive Temple of Maipú, a historic site linked to independence, as well as its parks and recreational areas such as Tres Poniente Park. The commune also has cultural centers, large shopping malls, schools, universities, and a transport network that connects it to the rest of the capital.
